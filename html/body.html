Assassin's Creed 1
"Laa shay'a waqui'n moutlaq bale kouloun moumkine. Nothing is true, everything is permitted" - Altaïr Ibn-La'Ahad

The Assassin's Creed series was born with this being its very first game in 2007. The game was released around November 2007 for PC, XBox 360 and PS3.

What's the plot?

It revolves around the life of Desmond Miles, a bartender kidnapped from New York by a Templar organisation, Abstergo Industries, and forced to relive the memories of Altaïr Ibn-La'Ahad, a Levantine assassin hailing from Masyaf, Syria during the Third Crusade.

Through the journey, he relives his memories of being an assassin in Masyaf, his tragic past, the unexpected event at the beginning which changed his life, and at last, how he was betrayed by his own Brotherhood.

What are the features of this game?

  • Go on missions to carry out assassinations of specific leaders
  • Blend with the crowd and strike silently with the new Hidden blade
  • A variety of moves, along with air-assassinations and chain-kills
  • Animus glitches, which may give you a new way of seeing things. You can come back down using a leap of faith
  • Travel to Damascus, Masyaf, Jerusalem and Acre
  • Eagle vision allows you to detect the target
  • Parkouring through the cities with an array of skills
  • Different types of missions, like pickpocketing, saving a citizen, etc.
  • Access the viewpoints to view the city like never before!

Assassin's Creed 2
"I did not choose this path. It was chosen for me. At first, I thought vengeance would be easy. For thirty years I've lived in the shadows, visited death upon those who deserved it, and vanished like the wind. I don't know who started this conspiracy, but I know who will end it. My name is Ezio Auditore da Firenze. Like my father before me, I am an Assassin." - Ezio Auditore da Firenze

One of the most popular games in the series, Assassin's Creed 2 precedes the famous Ezio Trilogy which consists of 3 games telling the tale of Ezio Auditore da Firenze, a Florentine assassin during the Renaissance period. The game was released around November 2009 for PS3, PS4 & XBox, March 2010 for PCs.

What's the plot?


Desmond Miles has escaped the Abstergo laboratory with an employee, Lucy Stillman to the secret warehouse at Rome where he meets two assassins, Shaun Hastings and Rebecca Crane. He then relieves the memories of the assassin, Ezio Auditore da Firenze residing in Firenze.
Ezio, at the age of 17, finds out that his father's friend had betrayed them and so, his father , unknowingly an assassin, and his two brothers are hanged for treason. He escapes the city with his mother and sister to the La Rosa Colta, where he learns survival tactics from Paola, the owner of the brothel. The party goes to Monterregioni, where Ezio's uncle, Mario Auditore resides.

Thus, he travels to various cities in Italy, confronting his conspirators and assassinating them, befriending many people, including Leonardo da Vinci . He eventually confronts the mastermind behind all the conspiracy, Rodrigo Borgia .

What are the features?

  • Several new locations to visit in Renaissance-era Italy
  • New eagle vision allows third-person view
  • A variety of weapons and customization
  • You can now swim, ride a horse, or even fly [with caution]!
  • Many more viewpoints to visit!>New different types of missions added
  • Upgrade your villa and get revenue from it [discounts included!]
  • Shops are open and now sell weapons, armoury, potions, or even dyes!
  • Improved combat system>New day-night cycle
  • Notoriety system and ways to reduce notoriety
  • Factions which can assist you in your journey

Assassin's Creed Brotherhood
"I thought my work was done. I was wrong. Once more, I must venture into the fray. By recruiting enemies of the state, we arm those who have been disarmed by the Borgia. The greed, the corruption, the tyranny my enemies have spread will burn to the ground. From the ashes of vengeance, a new Rome will rise." - Ezio Auditore da Firenze

Assassin's Creed Brotherhood is a direct sequel of Assassin's Creed 2. It was released in November 2010. It continues the journey of Ezio Auditore da Firenze fighting the Borgia in Rome.

What's the plot?

Desmond Miles, as we previously had seen, was relieving the memories of Ezio Auditore da Firenze to find the location of the Apple of Eden . He ended up finding himself in the memory of the Siege of Viana. He, then, is again forced into an earlier memory...
Ezio is in the Sistine Chapel with the Staff of Eden. Noticing that Rodrigo Borgia, he and Mario Auditore went to Monterregioni on horseback. Everything seemed fine and cozy, until Rodrigo's son, Cesare Borgia laid seige to Monterregioni.

Ezio finds himself at Rome tended to his wounds. Learning that his uncle is no more, he sets out to find his benefactor, Niccolò Machiavelli. Through him, he comes to know that Rome is in dispair under the Borgia. He travels and founds the Italian Brotherhood at Tiber Island.

The brotherhood, lead by him, begins destroying the Borgia influence, killing generals and eventually, a face-off with Cesare in the Siege of Viana.

What are the features?

  • Several new weapons, including an upgrade to the Hidden Blade
  • Visit the places and famous monuments in the city of Rome!
  • New multiplayer mode [Animi Training mode] allows players to assume the role of noted Templars and compete with each other
  • Upgrading sheath allows Ezio to carry heavy weapons
  • Horse mechanics improved; new horse combat system
  • Tunnels replace travel stations for fast travel
  • New recruitment system
  • Desmond can now interact with the team and explore present-day Monterregioni
  • You can renovate the entire Rome by freeing regions from Borgia control
  • New side-activities include Lairs of Romulus, special missions, Cristina's missions, etc.
Assassin's Creed Revelations
"I have lived my life to the best of my ability. But I have not been able to escape fate, anger, or pain. Bring me the answers and the road that leads to the truth. Reveal to me once and for all, how all of this will end." - Ezio Auditore da Firenze

Assassin's Creed Revelations is the final game in the Ezio Trilogy. It was released in November 2011. A direct sequel to Assassin's Creed Brotherhood , it shows us a late journey of Ezio Auditore da Firenze to Masyaf and later, Constantinople, where he aids the Ottoman Brotherhood.

What's the plot?

Desmond Miles is in a coma following the events of Assassin's Creed Brotherhood. He wakes up in the Animus Black Room and meets his predecessor, Clay Kaczmarek, who explains that he needs to get to a Sync Nexus through Ezio's memories to regain consciousness. Thus, he begins reliving the memories of Ezio Auditore da Firenze, who is now in Masyaf in search of the Great Library.

Ezio is in Masyaf in search of the Great Library. Guided by his late father's letterAltaïr's spirit, he finds out Templars in the area, planning to reach the Library as well. He manages to kill them and their head and boards a ship to Constantinople.
He is warmly received by the local Brotherhood, and finds out a hidden conspiracy against the Ottoman prince, Suleiman to destabilize the region. In search of the truth, he finds out mementos left by the master assassin and mentor, Altaïr and accounts left by Niccolò Polo.

What are the features?


  • Explore around the places in Constantinople [present-day Istanbul]
  • Have a hookblade? You can zip through the ziplines for speed!
  • New weapons and armoury include bombs, maces, etc.
  • Revamped eagle vision; eagle sense allows you to track enemies and targets, datura scent, etc.
  • Assassin den system introduced; Ezio must now defend the den if the notoriety becomes too high
  • Refined recruitment system allows recruits to form contracts
  • New enemies - stalkers, can attack Ezio at any time
  • Animus data fragments allow Desmond to access his previous memories
  • Revamped multiplayer mode along with credit system
Assassin's Creed 3
"On this land, I am torn. Part of me wants to fight and repel all outsiders. The other part of me is the outsider. In the name of liberty, I will fight the enemy regardless of their allegiance. While men of courage write the history of this day, the future of our land depends on those who are truly free." - Ratonhnhaké:ton | Connor Kenway

Assassin's Creed 3 is the 5th game in the series, after Assassin's Creed Revelations, and the first game in the Kenway Saga. It was released on October 2012 for PS3 and XBox & November 2012 for PCs. It shows us two different stories during the American Revolution and two different protagonists - Haytham Kenway and his son, Connor. Along with the main game, is its DLC - Tyranny of King Washington, giving the story a entirely new twist.
A remastered version of the game was launched in March 2019, adding new features and better graphics.

What's the plot?

Desmond Miles regains his consciousness and the team, consisting of William Miles, Rebecca and Shaun finds an ancient cave which led to the Grand Temple. He activates the equipment and a timer for the Second Disaster using the Apple of Eden. He then relieves some memories of Haytham Kenway, who was in London.

Haytham is in the London Theatre Royal, where he murders a man to steal his medallion. Then, he is dispatched to the British colonies in America to find out a storehouse of the Ones who Came Before. There, he finds five men devoted to his cause, and kills a slave trader to free members of the tribe, Kanien'kehá:ka. One of them, Kaniehtí:io agrees to locate the storehouse on the condition that he must kill Edward Braddock. They gradually develop romantic feelings for each other.

Kaniehtí:io's and Haytham's son, Ratonhnhaké:ton encounters the members of the Templar Order while playing hide and seek. Threatening him and knocking him unconscious, they set his village on fire, in which many die. Gaining information from the Piece of EdenHe sets out to meet Achilles Davenport, the mentor of the Colonial Brotherhood. After receiving training, he sets out to Boston and then New York in search of the ones who burnt his village and his father.

What are the features?

  • Fast travel mechanism allows you to quickly travel to various locations
  • Redesigned animus interface
  • Various methods of movement added; Connor can now slide under obstacles, climb trees, etc.
  • Overhauled fighting system; Connor can now wield two weapons simultaneously
  • New weapons and new feature of hidden blade, which makes it ejectable
  • Trading system now allows you to buy and sell resources from hunting
  • New naval system allows you to go on mission aboard the Aquila
  • Lairs of Romulus replaced by Peg-leg missions
  • Regenerative health system
  • New weather conditions include rain, snow, etc.
  • Connor can invite citizens to stay and build a community in the Davenport Homestead
Assassin's Creed 4 - Black Flag
"I once sailed for a king. I obeyed men of privilege and wealth. Now, I bend my knee to no man. My only oath is to my crew and together we will take back what is rightfully ours. With blood and steel, we shall stand up to the powerful. Captains will curse our flag, and kings will fear it. As long as empires generate wealth and riches, we will be there to bleed them dry." - Edward Kenway

Assassin's Creed 4 - Black Flag is the 6th game in the series and the second game in the Kenway Saga. Released in October 2013 for PS3 and XBox and November for PCs, it throws a light on the story of Haytham's father [and Connor's grandfather], Edward Kenway during the Golden Age of Piracy in the Carribean. The game also features a DLC - Freedom cry, featuring the quartermaster-turned assassin, Adéwalé.

What's the plot?

After collecting the DNA sample from Desmond Miles' corpse, Abstergo Entertainment hired a researcher, Noob, to relieve the memories of a pirate-turned assassin, Edward Kenway as a part of the Sample 17 Project.

In the beginning, we find a shipwrecked and stranded Edward along with an assassin, whom he kills and disguises as. In his pocket was a letter addressed to the Governor of Cuba, Laureano de Torres y Ayala. He travels to Havana in hopes of rewards, accompanied by a merchant, Stede Bonnet.

After meeting up with the Governor, he is formally inducted into the Order. They meet a person known as the Sage, and imprison him. However, Edward wasn't pleased with the reward and set him free. As a result, he was imprisoned with another prisoner named Adéwalé in a ship. The duo freed themselves and stole a brig, to name it the Jackdaw. They meet up with several famous pirates, including the threatening Blackbeard, and eventually confront Torres and the Sage.

What are the features?

  • Explore the seas and islands around the Carribean!
  • Drastic improvement in the naval system
  • Edward can now dive inside the sea to find treasures and hunt sea animals
  • Viewpoints now reveal information about the area
  • Improved dual-wielding system
  • Your crew will sing shanties while travelling in ship
  • Revamped naval combat system
  • New side missions include bar fights, assassin missions, etc.
  • You can upgrade Jackdaw and recuit crew members through various ways
  • Remake of the notoriety system now applicable worldwide
Assassin's Creed Rogue
"But in the end, it doesn't matter how history remembers me. What matters is that I follow my own Creed." - Shay Patrick Cormac

Assassin's Creed Rogue is the 7th game in the series and the last game in the Kenway Saga. Released in November 2014 for PS3 and XBox and March 2015 for PCs, it tells the story of assassin-turned templar Shay Cormac during the .
The remastered version came out in March 2018.

What's the plot?

A Helix researcher analyst is tasked to relive the memories of an assassin-turned templar, Shay Patrick Cormac. In the process, they triggered a virus, which alerted the facility and thus, they were pulled out from the animus for a brief period of time before reliving them again under their supervisor, Violet da Costa.

We are shown an assassin Shay chasing his friend and fellow assassin, Liam O'Brien . After hearing cannon fire, they run to the camp, where Louis-Joseph Gaultier, Chevalier de la Vérendrye is waiting for them. A fist fight ensues, which is stopped by Liam. Shay makes his way to the British camp, kills their soldiers and claims their ship [and names it the Morrigan]. They helped Chevalier's ship, the Gerfaut .

The party soon arrives at the Davenport Homestead and discuss matters with Achilles Davenport, Kesegowaase, Hope Jensen, and Adéwalé. Shay gets the mission to assassinate two influential Templars in the Order, and retrieve the Manuscript and the Precursor Box . After killing them, he set out to help Benjamin Franklin at the Sleepy Hollow to activate the box. With this, they learnt that the Precursor site is at Lisbon, Portugal.
Shay travels to Lisbon and activate the artifact, only to see it disintegrate and result in an earthquake which led to a huge loss of life and property. Devastated to see the accident, he confronts his mentor, who turns him off. Seeing that the others won't listen to his reason, Shay sneaked into the room and stole the Manuscript, but was discovered by Achilles. He tried to flee and arrived at a dead-end, and was shot by Liam.


Shay finds himself in the care of the Finnigans at New York. He set out to eliminate a gang in the city and makes acquaintance with colonel George Monro. He also rescued Christopher Gist, who'd soon be his quartermaster, and reclaimed the Morrigan. Soon, he made acquaintance with the Grandmaster of the templar Order, Haytham Kenway and decided to track and hunt down his former fellow assassins and eliminate the assassin presence in the Americas.

What are the features

  • Explore around North Atlantic and the Arctic!
  • Sea shanties return!
  • Naval combat upgraded; new weapons include Puckel gun
  • Gang headquarters make a presence in cities; eliminating them gives income
  • Stalkers return; they are now permanent after leaving the Brotherhood
  • Environment hazards include freezing water, poison gas, etc.
  • New weapons include air rifle and grenade launcher
  • Assassin Interception missions introduced
  • Shay can now kill civilians and animals without getting desynchronized after leaving the Brotherhood
  • New sea animals include narwhals, white whales, etc.
Assassin's Creed Unity
"It is sometimes hard to remember how easy life was, before the world changed. Now people are starving in the streets and those with the will to fight back are imprisoned... or worse. The life I had is gone. The world I knew with it. Amongst the violence, amongst the chaos, I will find justice no matter the cost. If we've been betrayed from within, I have to do this myself." - Arno Victor Dorian

Assassin's Creed Unity is the 8th game in the series, and the first game in the Initiate Duology. Set in the 18th-century Paris, France during the French Revolution, it tells the story of Arno Dorian, an assassin raised in a templar family. The game was released on November 2014 [the same year as Assassin's Creed Rogue] for PCs, XBox and PS4. The game comes along with its DLC - Dead Kings, featuring Saint-Denis.

What's the plot?

We enter the game as a Helix Initiate user playing the memory of a templar under the service of Jacques de Molay. The user is interrupted by an assassin, Bishop, who explained that they were being brainwashed by Abstergo Entertainment, and suggested to relive the memories of an assassin, to get to know about the conflict between them.

Arno Dorian and his father are in the Hall of Mirrors, Versailles Palce. He meets a girl named Élise de la Serre. A commotion insues, and Arno sifts through the crowd to see his father dead. Soon after the incident, François de la Serre adopts him.

Years later, Arno goes to a party. After much complications and sneaking, he meets Élise and shares a kiss with her. Interrupted by a guard, he turns to leave, only to find his guardian, François de la Serre, dead. Charles Gabriel Sivert confronts him and frames him for murdering him. Arno is soon arrested and sent to the Bastille. He makes acquaintances with a man named Pierre Bellec , who informs him that his late father was an assassin. They manage to escape as a result of the Storming of the Bastille.

Arno went to Élise, who handed him a letter and warned him of being betrayed by the other templars. He and Bellec went to the Assassin Council, where he was inducted into the Parisian Brotherhood. With this, he begins on a journey to find out the main conspirator of François de la Serre's killer, amidst the Revolution.

What are the features?

  • Next-level graphics with crowd rendering
  • Explore the city of Paris and Saint-Denis
  • Campaign co-op introduced; co-op experience greatly revamped
  • Customization available for the gear
  • A variety of weapons, include the famous Phantom Blade
  • Adaptive mission mechanism allows you to approach missions in different way
  • Assassination missions from AC 1 return as black-box missions
  • Drastic changes in parkouring and stealth
  • Improvements in combat system
